EC-Council Certified Ethical Hacking v12

The Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) credential stands as the most trusted ethical hacking certification globally, highly recommended by employers. It represents the most sought-after information security certification and is crucial for professionals in critical infrastructure and essential service providers. Established in 2003, CEH is a recognized standard within the information security community. CEH v12 continually integrates the latest hacking techniques, advanced tools, and exploits used by hackers and information security professionals today. The core mission of CEH remains relevant: "To beat a hacker, you need to think like a hacker."
